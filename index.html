HOW IT WORKS

At Brain to Body, our process begins with understanding you.

When you visit us, we take a comprehensive medical history (blood reports required) and measure key physical parameters like height, weight, and body composition. We also delve into your daily routines, preferences, and any limitations you may have.

Together, with your active participation, Aruna and Soumya craft a personalized diet plan, physical exercise regime, and mental health program designed to make minimal changes to your existing habits. With the weekly catch-up calls, the plans get updated.

Weekly check-ins are recommended until you reach your health goals. We strongly advise people to invest their time, energy, and money in long-term plans as health and mind makeover is a slow process.

Once you've achieved your goals, we guide you on maintaining your results independently. You'll learn how to:

  • Plan balanced meals.
  • Understand portion sizes.
  • Identify red flags in your diet.
